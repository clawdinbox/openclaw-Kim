# Marcel Melzig — Ebook Design Style Guide
*Extracted from existing products, Feb 2026*

## Products Analyzed
1. **"How to Start a Fashion Brand Without an Agency"** — 24 pages, A4 (595×842pt), ~3MB
2. **"The Global Luxury Resale Market Guide"** — 16 pages, A4 (595×842pt), ~354KB

---

## Typography System

### Font Family: **Montserrat** (exclusively)
| Weight | Usage | Sizes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| ExtraBold | Cover title, author name | 12–15pt |
| Bold | Section titles, headings | 14–58pt |
| BoldItalic | Section subheadings | 23pt |
| SemiBold | Subtitle, credentials | 10.5–12pt |
| Medium | Cover subtitle | 17pt |
| Regular | Body text, bullets | 12–23pt |

### Secondary Font
- **StarShield-Regular** — decorative/display font used at large sizes (77–89pt) on cover pages only

### Type Hierarchy
1. **Page title** — Montserrat Bold, 47–58pt, black or cyan
2. **Section heading** — Montserrat BoldItalic, 23pt, cyan (#00ADEE)
3. **Body text** — Montserrat Regular, 12pt, black
4. **Page numbers** — top-left, Montserrat Regular, 12pt

---

## Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| White | #FFFFFF | Background, reversed text on dark elements |
| Black | #000000 | Body text, some headings |
| Cyan/Teal | #00ADEE / #00AEEF | Accent headings, section labels, highlight bars |
| Deep Blue Gradient | ~#0066B3 → #023E8A | Cover backgrounds, header/footer bars |

**3-color system**: White + Black + Cyan. Clean, professional, modern.

---

## Layout Patterns

### Cover Page
- Blue gradient background (top-to-bottom, deep blue → cyan)
- Background image with overlay (hands/data visualization)
- Title: large condensed all-caps, center-aligned
- Key words highlighted with **colored background bars** (black or cyan pill-shapes)
- Subtitle in rounded rectangle banner
- Author section: circular headshot photo + name + credentials
- Author name in white bold, title in cyan

### Interior Pages
- **White background** for all content pages
- **Dark navy header bar** at top of each page
- **Page number** in top-left corner (02, 03, etc.)
- **Left-aligned single column** layout
- **Title block**: large bold text (2-4 lines), stacked tight
  - First line(s) in **black**
  - Last line(s) in **cyan** (#00ADEE)
- **Thin horizontal rule** divider between title and content
- **Diagonal/triangular corner element** in bottom-right (dark navy with page number)
- Generous margins (~20% of page width)

### Content Pages
- Section headings in BoldItalic, cyan
- Body text 12pt Regular, comfortable line-height (~1.5)
- Bullet points with standard markers
- Occasional **2-column layouts** for comparison/grid content
- Numbered items in **blue gradient boxes** with white reversed text

### Final Page (CTA)
- Full dark background
- Large white text, center-aligned
- "FOLLOW ME ON LINKEDIN" / "LET'S TRANSFORM INSIGHTS INTO ACTION"
- LinkedIn URL or CTA in cyan
- Clean, bold closing

---

## Visual Elements
- **Gradient backgrounds** (blue spectrum) for cover + accent sections
- **Circular author photo** with subtle border
- **Background bar highlights** on key title words (pill/label style)
- **Diagonal geometric shapes** (triangles/corners) as page decorations
- **No emoji, no clipart, no stock icons**
- **Minimal imagery** — mostly typography-driven design
- When images appear: professional, abstract, tech/data visualization

---

## Overall Aesthetic
- **Clean, modern, professional**
- **Montserrat-driven** — geometric sans-serif gives tech/business feel
- **Blue/cyan brand color** — consistent across both products
- **Left-aligned, generous whitespace**
- **Typography as design** — large stacked titles ARE the visual impact
- **Consultant/analyst positioning** — McKinsey-lite, not startup-bro
- **Minimal decoration** — let content breathe

---

## Key Design Rules for Future Ebooks
1. Use Montserrat family exclusively (Bold, Regular, BoldItalic minimum)
2. Accent color: #00ADEE cyan
3. Page titles: large stacked text, black + cyan split
4. White interior pages with dark header/footer bars
5. Cover: blue gradient + background image + highlight bars on key words
6. Always include: author name, circular photo, credentials
7. Page numbers formatted as 2-digit (02, 03...) top-left
8. Final page = full dark background CTA
9. A4 format (595×842pt)
10. No emojis, no hashtags, no casual elements
