32% of Gen Z closets are resale items. Now TikTok Shop — 150M US users, 88 minutes daily average (per company reporting) — is entering the authenticated luxury game.

Vestiaire Collective IPO ambitions for 2025. The RealReal's public market struggles. StockX and GOAT professionalizing authentication. And now TikTok wants in.

The brands that survive won't fight resale. They'll architect it — controlled release of authenticated pre-owned through preferred partners, with revenue share.

Your 2025 channel strategy needs a "resale" line item. Not as an afterthought. As a primary growth pillar. The platforms are coming whether you invite them or not.
