# Ebook Promotion Plan: AI's Impact on Fashion

## Phase 1: Soft Launch (Week 1)

### Day 0 — Pre-launch
- [ ] Post LinkedIn poll: "What's your biggest AI question for fashion brands?" (engagement bait + research)
- [ ] Tease on X/Threads: "Dropping something next week for brand leaders wrestling with AI decisions"

### Day 1 — Launch
- [ ] **LinkedIn main post** — Long-form announcement with PDF preview image
  - Hook: "I spent 3 months analyzing how AI is reshaping fashion's value chain"
  - CTA: Link to Gumroad + mention lead magnet
  - Pin to profile for 2 weeks
  
- [ ] **X post** — Punchy version, same link
- [ ] **Substack** — Send to newsletter list with excerpt + Gumroad link

### Day 2-3 — Momentum
- [ ] Reply to every comment on LinkedIn launch post
- [ ] Share in 2-3 relevant LinkedIn groups (Fashion Network, etc.)
- [ ] X/Threads: Quote tweets with insights from the playbook

### Day 4-7 — Content Extraction
- [ ] LinkedIn: Carousel post — "5 Shifts from the AI Fashion Playbook" (extract from lead magnet)
- [ ] X: Thread on one section (e.g., "How AI changes demand planning")
- [ ] Substack Notes: Short insight + link

## Phase 2: Sustained Push (Weeks 2-4)

### Weekly Content Cadence

**Monday — LinkedIn Post**
- Extract one framework from playbook (AI Flywheel, 90-Day Sprint, etc.)
- Give away 80%, sell the 20% depth
- CTA: "Full framework in the playbook [link]"

**Wednesday — LinkedIn Poll or Question**
- Engagement play to stay visible
- Example: "Which AI use case are you most skeptical about?"

**Thursday — Newsletter**
- Market Pulse newsletter includes playbook mention in footer
- Or dedicate one edition to AI topic with soft CTA

**Friday — Linkedin Post**
- Social proof or case study angle
- Example: "3 brands getting AI right (and what they're doing)"

**Daily (X/Threads/Substack Notes/Instagram)**
- One insight from playbook, posted at scheduled times
- 15:00 X, 17:00 Substack, 19:00 Threads, 21:00 Instagram

## Phase 3: Expansion (Month 2+)

### Guest Appearances
- [ ] Pitch yourself to 3-5 fashion/retail podcasts
- [ ] Offer to write guest posts for industry newsletters (Lean Luxe, Fashion and Beauty Monitor)
- [ ] LinkedIn Live or Twitter Space on AI in fashion

### Partnerships
- [ ] Offer affiliate/rev-share to fashion consultants who serve your target audience
- [ ] Cross-promote with complementary Gumroad creators
- [ ] Reach out to 10 LinkedIn connections who work in fashion strategy — ask for feedback, not sales

### Lead Magnet Funnel
- [ ] Pin lead magnet link to LinkedIn featured section
- [ ] Add lead magnet CTA to LinkedIn bio
- [ ] Include in email signature
- [ ] Create simple landing page (Carrd or Gumroad free product) to collect emails

## Content Angles to Rotate

1. **Fear/Gap** — "Most fashion brands are unprepared for what's coming"
2. **Opportunity** — "The AI flywheel: how to compound your advantage"
3. **Proof** — Case studies of brands using AI well (Nike, Zara, Shein)
4. **Process** — "How I researched this" (builds authority)
5. **Specific** — Deep dive on one section (e.g., "AI in demand planning: the real numbers")
6. **Social Proof** — Screenshots of early buyer feedback

---

## Lead Magnet → Paid Conversion Funnel

1. **Free download** → Automated email sequence (3 emails over 7 days)
   - Email 1: Deliver PDF + quick win tip
   - Email 2: Deep dive on one shift + soft pitch
   - Email 3: Case study + direct pitch to full playbook

2. **Retargeting** (if you run ads)
   - Pixel on Gumroad
   - Retarget lead magnet downloaders with paid product
