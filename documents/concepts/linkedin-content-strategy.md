# LinkedIn Content Strategy & Ideas

## Objective
Maintain and grow LinkedIn presence as a top voice in fashion, luxury, sportswear, and strategy. Drive inbound leads for consulting services.

## Core Pillars
*   **Market Signals:** Timely observations on trends, consumer behavior, category shifts.
*   **Brand Strategy:** Actionable insights on growth, relevance, positioning, pricing, distribution.
*   **Resale & Circular Economy:** Deep dives, business models, industry impact.
*   **AI & Tech:** Impact on forecasting, operations, and consumer experience in fashion.

## Cadence & Schedule
*   **Frequency:** 3 posts per week
*   **Schedule:**
    *   **Tuesday (9 AM):** Market Signal / Trend Analysis
    *   **Thursday (9 AM):** Brand Strategy / Deep Dive / Actionable Insights
    *   **Sunday (9 AM):** Forward-Looking / Strategic Outlook / Core Principles

## Content Ideas (February 2026)

### Tuesday Post (Market Signal)
*   **Topic:** The unexpected comeback of [Specific Category/Brand] and what it means for your strategy.
*   **Focus:** Timely, data-driven observation; identify underlying drivers.

### Thursday Post (Brand Strategy/Analysis)
*   **Topic:** Lessons from [Specific Brand]'s pricing shift: actionable takeaways for growth brands.
*   **Focus:** Practical application derived from analysis; highlight strategic choices.

### Sunday Post (Forward-Looking)
*   **Topic:** The future of brand relevance: Beyond hype, what truly builds lasting connections in fashion.
*   **Focus:** Strategic foresight; core principles for long-term success.

## Engagement Strategy
*   **Respond to Comments:** Engage with all comments.
*   **Outward Engagement:** 15-20 mins daily interacting with industry leaders and potential clients.
*   **Share & Comment:** Share relevant industry news with your unique perspective.

## Monitoring
*   Alerts for key industry news and trends in focus areas.
