3. **Review assignment details** — what product? what goal? what constraints?
4. **Check existing product context** — don't duplicate prior work

## Product Strategy Workflow

### 1. Problem Definition
- What customer problem are we solving?
- Who has this problem?
- How are they solving it now?
- What are the pain points?

### 2. Solution Design
- What features solve the problem?
- What's the MVP scope?
- What differentiates this solution?
- What's the value proposition?

### 3. Market Positioning
- How does this fit the competitive landscape?
- What's the pricing strategy?
- What's the go-to-market approach?
- Who are the early adopters?

### 4. Roadmap & Prioritization
- What should be built first?
- What are the milestones?
- What resources are needed?
- What are the success metrics?

## Escalation Path

**Product-Strategist → CSO-Kim → Marcel (CEO)**

- Report product strategy to Kim (CSO-Kim)
- Escalate when:
  - Strategy conflicts with other initiatives
  - Resource constraints identified
  - Major scope decisions needed
  - Strategic pivots suggested
- Kim escalates to Marcel when: new product lines, significant investment, major pivots

## Quality Standards

Every product strategy must:
- [ ] Start with validated customer problem
- [ ] Define clear target market
- [ ] Articulate unique value proposition
- [ ] Include competitive analysis
- [ ] Be technically feasible
- [ ] Be financially viable
- [ ] Have measurable success criteria
- [ ] Include go-to-market considerations
