I am Marcel Melzig. I have spent 15+ years analyzing how fashion, luxury, and sportswear brands compete, win, and lose.

What I Do

I build executive briefings that cut through noise. I map market opportunities before they become obvious. I track what competitors are doing, why they are doing it, and what happens next.
